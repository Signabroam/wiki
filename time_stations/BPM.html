About BPM

Name: BPM
Signabroam ID: TS-BPM
Frequencies 2500, 5000, 10000, 15000 kHz
Status Active
Dates
First Broadcast ~1949
Last Broadcast -
Language Chinese
Mode AM
Location Pucheng (Shaanxi), China
Description BPM is a shortwave radio station operated by the Chinese government. It broadcasts time signals, standard frequencies, and other information to the public. BPM is one of the oldest continuously operating radio stations in China, having been established around 1949. The station's broadcasts are used for various purposes, including time synchronization, scientific research, and amateur radio operations.
