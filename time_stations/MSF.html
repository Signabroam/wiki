About MSF

Name: MSF
Signabroam ID: TS-MSF
Frequencies 60 kHz
Status Active
Dates
First Broadcast 1950
Last Broadcast -
Language N/A
Mode ASK / PWM
Location Anthorn, Cumbria, England, United Kingdom
Description MSF is a time signal station located in Anthorn, Cumbria, England, United Kingdom. It is operated by the National Physical Laboratory (NPL) and provides accurate time and frequency information to users in the UK and surrounding areas. The station transmits a continuous signal on 60 kHz, which can be received by radio-controlled clocks and other devices that require precise timing.
