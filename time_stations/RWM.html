About RWM

Name: RWM
Signabroam ID: TS-RWM
Frequencies 4996, 9996, 14996 kHz
Status Active
Dates
First Broadcast ~1965-1970
Last Broadcast -
Language N/A
Mode OOK, CW
Location Moscow, Russia
Description RWM is a Russian time signal station operated by the Russian government. It broadcasts time signals and other information to the public. RWM is used for various purposes, including time synchronization, scientific research, and radio clocks.
