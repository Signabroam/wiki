About CHU

Name: CHU
Signabroam ID: TS-CHU
Frequencies 3330, 7850, 14670 kHz
Status Imminent incoming Shutdown
Dates
First Broadcast 1923
Last Broadcast June 22nd, 2026 between 14:00 and 15:00 UTC
Language English, French (Quebec)
Mode AM, USB with carrier
Location Canada
Description CHU is a shortwave radio station operated by the National Research Council of Canada (NRC). It broadcasts time signals, standard frequencies, and other information to the public. CHU is one of the oldest continuously operating radio stations in the world, having been established in 1923. The station's broadcasts are used for various purposes, including time synchronization, scientific research, and amateur radio operations.
