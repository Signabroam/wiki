Description WWVH is a shortwave radio station operated by the National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) in the United States. It broadcasts time signals, standard frequencies, and other information to the public. WWVH is one of the oldest continuously operating radio stations in the world, having been established in 1919. The station's broadcasts are used for various purposes, including time synchronization, scientific research, and amateur radio operations.
