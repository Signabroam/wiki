About ALS162

Name: ALS162, TDF, FI
Signabroam ID: TS-ALS162
Frequencies 162 kHz
Status Active
Dates

July 1980, moved to 162 kHz on the center carrier.
Last Broadcast -
Language French (ceased audio operations in 2016)
Mode AM + CW, phase modulation DCF77 Compatible time frame
Location Allouis, France
Description ALS162 is a longwave time signal and standard-frequency radio station operated by TDF (Télédiffusion de France). It is located in Allouis, France. The station broadcasts time signals and standard frequencies, which are used for various applications, including synchronizing clocks and watches, as well as for scientific research and other purposes requiring precise timekeeping. The station has been in operation since 1977 and continues to provide accurate time signals to the public over the old 162 kHz France Inter center carrier.
