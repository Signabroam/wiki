About HBG

Name: HBG
Signabroam ID: TS-HBG
Frequencies 75 kHz
Status Inactive
Dates
First Broadcast 1966
Last Broadcast 2012
Language N/A
Mode AM time code
Location Switzerland
Description HBG was a low-frequency time signal station operated by the Swiss Federal Office of Metrology (METAS) in Switzerland. It transmitted time signals on 75 kHz frequency, providing accurate time information to users in Switzerland and surrounding areas. The station ceased operations in 2012 and is no longer active, the station got replaced by DCF77 from Germany.
