About LOL

Name: LOL
Signabroam ID: TS-LOL
Frequencies 5000 10000, 15000 kHz
Status Inactive
Dates
First Broadcast ~1950
Last Broadcast ~1990s
Language Argentinean
Mode AM, DSB
Location Argentina
Description LOL was a high-frequency time signal station operated by the Argentinean government in Argentina. It transmitted time signals on 5000, 10000, and 15000 kHz frequencies, providing accurate time information to users in Argentina and surrounding areas. The station ceased operations in the 1990s and is no longer active.
