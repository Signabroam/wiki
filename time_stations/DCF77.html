About DCF77

Name: DCF77
Signabroam ID: TS-DCF77
Frequencies 77.5 kHz
Status Active
Dates
First Broadcast ~1959
Last Broadcast -
Language N/A
Mode AM ASK 100/200ms Pulses, PN code phase modulation
Location Mainflinger Str. 1, 63533 Mainhausen, Germany
Description DCF77 is a longwave time signal and standard-frequency radio station. It is operated by the Physikalisch-Technische Bundesanstalt (PTB), the national metrology institute of Germany, and is located in Mainflingen, about 25 km (16 mi) south-east of Frankfurt am Main. The transmitter continuously broadcasts time signals, which are used for synchronizing clocks and watches, as well as for other applications requiring precise timekeeping.
