About JJY (HF)

Name: JJY (HF)
Signabroam ID: TS-JJY-HF
Frequencies 2500, 5000, 8000, 10000 kHz
Status Inactive
Dates
First Broadcast ~1940
Last Broadcast 2001
Language Japanese
Mode AM, DSB
Location Japan
Description JJY (HF) was a high-frequency time signal station operated by the National Institute of Information and Communications Technology (NICT) in Japan. It transmitted time signals on multiple frequencies, including 2500, 5000, 8000, and 10000 kHz. The station provided accurate time information to users in Japan and surrounding areas. However, it ceased operations in 2001 and is no longer active.
