About JJY (LF)

Name: JJY (LF)
Signabroam ID: TS-JJY-LF
Frequencies 40, 60 kHz
Status Active
Dates
First Broadcast 1999
Last Broadcast -
Language N/A
Mode ASK / PWM
Location Japan
Description JJY (LF) is a low-frequency time signal station operated by the National Institute of Information and Communications Technology (NICT) in Japan. It transmits time signals on 40 kHz and 60 kHz frequencies, providing accurate time information to users in Japan and surrounding areas. The station is still active and continues to serve as a reliable source of time signals for various applications.
