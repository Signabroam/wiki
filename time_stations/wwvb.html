About WWVB

Name: WWVB
Signabroam ID: TS-WWVB
Frequencies 60 kHz
Dates
First Broadcast 1956
Last Broadcast -
Language N/A
Mode Atomic PWM x ASK
Location Fort Collins, Colorado, USA
Description WWVB is a shortwave radio station operated by the National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) in the United States. It broadcasts time signals. WWVB is one of the oldest continuously operating radio stations in the world, having been established in 1956. The station's broadcasts are used for various purposes, including time synchronization, scientific research, and radio clock.
