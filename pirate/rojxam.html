About Radio ROJX AM

Name: Radio ROJX AM
Signabroam ID: P6210
Frequencies 6210, 7680, 7510, 7265 kHz
Status Active
Voice Male (Live)
Mode AM (6 kHz)
Location France
Related Stations FUTS - France Universal Time Signal
Description Radio ROJX AM is a pirate station operated from France. It is a not very active station, and it can be heard on 6210 kHz and sometimes on 7510 kHz when the propagation on 6210 kHz is bad.
The station is known for its unique and entertaining content, which includes high varierty music genre. The station's slogan is "The Shortwave Radio Station of the Real Hits!" related to its diverse programming.
The station also have a FM version named as MazeFM (formerly FemboyFM), which is used to broadcast the same content as the shortwave station to local listeners. (the FM frequency is also pirate...)
