About Radio Mi Amigo International

Name: Radio Mi Amigo International
Signabroam ID: P1620
Frequencies 1620, 5775, 6085 kHz
Status Active
Voice Male
Mode AM (5 kHz)
Location Netherlands
Description Radio Mi Amigo International is a pirate station based in the Netherlands. It transmits large amounts of musics and announcements on the ID Radio Mi Amigo International.
