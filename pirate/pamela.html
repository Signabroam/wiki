About Radio Pamela

Name: Radio Pamela
Signabroam ID: P6935
Frequencies 6935 kHz
Status Active
Voice Male
Mode AM (5 kHz)
Location North West, Ireland
Description Radio Pamela is a pirate station that can be heard on the west coast of Ireland. It transmit large amounts of music and announcements on the ID Radio Pamela and also DJ.
