About the 45m Band

Name: The 45m Band
Signabroam ID: P6660
Frequencies 6400-6765 kHz
Status Active
Voice Male
Mode LSB (2-6 kHz)
Location France / Germany / Belgium / Netherlands / Italy / Spain
Description The 45m band is a shortwave frequency band used for international broadcasting. It is utilized by various pirate stations and commercial broadcasters. The band is known for its wide coverage and is often used for music, QSO, and other types of programming. Some of the notable stations that transmit on the 45m band include France Liberté transmitting on 6660 and 6670 kHz that can be heard from France all around the Europe since 1980, [EC] "Echo Charlie" pirates active since 2014, and many more. The 45m band is a popular choice for pirate radio stations due to its ability to reach a wide audience across Europe and beyond and is also concidered as a FreeBand (but this band isn't a FreeBand, it's originaly the 6 MHz Aviation and Maritime band).
