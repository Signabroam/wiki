About Radio Augusta International

Name: Radio Augusta International
Signabroam ID: P6140
Frequencies 6140 kHz
Status Active
Voice Male
Mode AM (5 kHz)
Location Netherlands (Via Radio Onda)
Ivory Coast
Description Radio Augusta International is a french pirate station based in the Netherlands. It transmits large amounts of musics, DJ Set and shows, this radio is transmit via the Radio Onda transmitter on 6140 kHz and sometimes there own transmitter.
The jingle of the station is "The best songs on the Radio, my favorite station is Radio Augusta".
