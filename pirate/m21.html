About Mystery for Joy

Name: Mystery for Joy
Signabroam ID: P21
Frequencies 3940, 4875, 4880, 4885, 4890, 4895, 5800, 6045 kHz
Status Active
Voice Male (Live)
Mode AM (5 kHz)
Location Operator: France
Transmitter: Germany
Description "Mystery for Joy, the best station in the world" is a pirate station operated by Agent21 and is operated from Germany and The Netherlands. It is a very active station, and it can be heard on many frequencies. The station is known for its unique and entertaining content, which includes music, talk shows, and comedy sketches. The station's slogan is "The best station in the world," and it has a loyal following of listeners who tune in regularly to enjoy its programming.

All information above is credited from Signabroam Shortwave Wiki and Agent21.
