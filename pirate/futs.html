About FUTS

Name: FUTS - France Universal Time Signal
Signabroam ID: P5020
Frequencies 5020, 10020 kHz
Status Active (Barely/Rarely)
Voice Male (TTS)
Mode AM (5 kHz), USB with carrier (5 kHz)
Location France (via ROJX AM)
Related Stations Radio ROJX AM
Description FUTS is a French pirate time station that broadcasts the time in French. It is a rare station that is only active for a few minutes or hours at a time. It is also known for its TTS voice in French and in English using the WWV voice.
The station is a mix of NIST WWV and CHU Canada...
There is a website where you can actually listen to the station online, is it active 24/7 because the website play the station in Local with a script.
The Website is availabe at https://futs.duckdns.org/ (Website owned by us, Signabroam.ovh)
