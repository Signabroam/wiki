About Radio Astra

Name: Radio Astra
Signabroam ID: P6270
Frequencies 4935 (via SimTX), 6070 (via Radio Channel292), 6270 kHz
Status Active
Voice Male (Live)
Mode AM
Location France (Via the old ROJX AM Transmitter)
Germany (Via Radio Channel292) (soon)
SimTX (Location: Northern Germany)
Related Stations FUTS - France Universal Time Signal Radio ROJX AM
Description Radio Astra, formerly known as Radio ROJX AM, is a pirate station that was originally broadcasted from France and is now broadcasted by RUCC on SimTX and on Radio Channel292 on real Shortwave. The station has been known for its unique and experimental content, which includes music and experimental programming. The station's slogan is "The Real Shortwave Station"
The station is located in France, but it is expected to be broadcasted from Germany in the future via Radio Channel292.
The station is also available on SimTX, a shortwave radio simulator, which allows listeners to tune in from anywhere in the world. More informations at https://simtx.net/ and on the wiki at https://wiki.simtx.net/index.php/Main_Page, where you can find more information about the station and its programming.
Radio Atra also have his own dedicated wiki on SimTX, all the informations on this one is related to SimTX and not to the real station. Available at https://wiki.simtx.net/index.php/Astra.
