About Richard "Dick" Bulger and his station

Name: Dick Bulger Radio
Signabroam ID: P6970
Frequencies 6970, 27000-27500 (11m CB) kHz
Status Active
Voice Male
Mode USB
Location Boston/Out of Boston, Massachusetts, USA
Description

Dick Bulger is a chaotic Boston-area radio presence whose late-night AM/SSB transmissions blend CB/ham radio ranting, conspiracy-fueled commentary, and unexpected anti-authoritarian insights. Raised near Revere Beach, he built a makeshift station around a clutter of coax cables, broken scanners, and a greasy mixer, broadcasting from what he calls his “mobile operations center.”

Although he began obsessed with Fox News and Alex Jones, his on-air style evolved into a strange mix of sports talk, conspiracy show, and accidental defense of workers’ rights. He is known for hijacking his own rants to complain about corporate greed, authoritarian clowns, and a rough-edged version of solidarity.

Unpolished and unpredictable, Dick Bulger is a loud, messy, roast-beef-powered figure who somehow became an accidental anti-fascist voice on the airwaves.
