About CIS-3

Name: CIS-3
Signabroam ID: MD3.3
Frequencies Multiple
Status Active
Voice Male, Female
Mode USB, 14/15 Channels (4/5 Groups of 2/3 Channels)
Location Russia
Description The CIS-3 also known at the 3 Tone circuit, is a 3 channel USB signal. It is a 3 channel signal that is used for unknown purpose, the channels have a 60 TPM (Tick Per Seconds) wave like a clock or something similar. The Purpose is unknown... Is it also used on many Southern Russian Networks station as a Channel Marker.
