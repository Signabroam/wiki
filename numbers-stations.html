Numbers Stations

Numbers stations are a type of shortwave radio station that broadcast coded messages, often in the form of sequences of numbers, letters, or Morse code. These stations have been used for espionage and intelligence purposes, and their broadcasts are typically aimed at specific recipients who possess the necessary decryption keys.

The Numbers Stations has been monitored since ~1990 by the ENIGMA (European Numbers Information Gathering and Monitoring Association), the group has been dissociate in 1999 and a new group has been formed in 2000 by fans of Shortwave under the name ENIGMA 2000 owned by Signalshed.
