About the Chinese Robot (VC01)

Name: Chinese Robot (VC01)
Frequencies Frequencies constantly changing - List of known frequencies
Status Active
Voice Female
Mode LSB, USB
Location China
Description Chinese Robot is a nickname of a station believed to be utilized by People's Liberation Army Air Force. It has been active since 2000.
