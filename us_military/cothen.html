About COTHEN

Name: Customs Over The Horizon Enforcement Network (COTHEN)
Signabroam ID: US-MIL-COTHEN
Operator United States Customs and Border Protection
Frequencies 5732, 7527, 8983 kHz, and others
Status Active
Mode USB
Use Counter-narcotics operations, maritime and aerial surveillance
Description COTHEN is a US customs HF network that supports law enforcement communications over long distances. Many HF enthusiasts include it in US government-related HF networks.
