About PJK Suffisant Curacao (ASW Operations)

Name: PJK Suffisant Curacao (ASW Operations)
Signabroam ID: D04
Frequencies 11178 kHz
Status Active
Voice Unknown
Mode USB
Location Willemstad ,Curacao, Netherlands Antilles
Known counterpart stations Dutch Navy, PJC NRN Hato Curacao (Counter-Narco OPS)
Description PJK is a station located in Curacao in the Netherlands Antilles located in the Caribbean islands. It is used by the Dutch Navy for ASW operations in the Caribbean. It is active since at least 2010, and it is still active.
