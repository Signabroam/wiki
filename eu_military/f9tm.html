About F9TM

Name: F9TM
Signabroam ID: MO23
Frequencies 3536 kHz
Status Active (Only the Thursday at 19:30 UTC+02 and last ~1h40 long)
Voice N/A
Mode CW
Location Ile-de-France, France
Known counterpart stations FAV22
Description F9TM is a CW station used by the "Centre National de Mise en Œuvre des Télécommunications Spatiales et Radioélectriques/Centre de Contrôle des Fréquences" (CNMO-TSR/CCF), its counterpart of the FAV22 CW station. It is active since at least 1948, and it is still active.
More informations at: https://web.r-e-f.org/le-reseau-f9tm-et-fav22/
