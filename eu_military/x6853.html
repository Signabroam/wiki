About XF Faders / XM Whales

Name: XF Faders / XM Whales
Signabroam ID: U6853
Frequencies 6853 kHz
Status Active
Voice Unknown
Mode USB
Location Somewhere in Europe
Known counterpart stations Unknown
Description This station first appeared in 2025 is a mysterious shortwave station that has been observed transmitting on 6853 kHz. It is known for the transmitted LoopBack audio (Receiver and Transmitter listening on the same frequency) making it sound like a "fading" or "whale-like" sound. The station's purpose and origin remain unknown, but it has been speculated to be a military or intelligence-related operation due to its unique transmission characteristics and on-air presence.
