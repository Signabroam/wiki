About One Beep, Two Beeps

Name: One Beep, Two Beeps
Callsigns: LBA, LBJ, JWG, JWT, JWZ, JXU
Signabroam ID: U4508
Frequencies 4508, 4500, 5099, 6405, 6429
Status Active
Voice Unknown
Mode USB, MIL-STD-4285
Location Bodø, Norway
Known counterpart stations NATO Stanags, Norwegian Navy
Description One Beep, Two Beeps is a nickname given to a signal emitted by the Norwegian Navy communications center in Bodø (callsigns: LBA, LBJ, JWG, JWT, JWZ, JXU). As implied by the name, it consists of noise with occasional 760 Hz beeps, one or more at a time.
