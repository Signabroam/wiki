About FAV22

Name: FAV22
Signabroam ID: MO22
Frequencies 3881, 6825 kHz (3880, 6824 kHz in USB)
Status Active
Voice N/A
Mode CW
Location Ile-de-France, France
Known counterpart stations F9TM
Description FAV22 is a CW station used by the "Centre National de Mise en œuvre Télécommunications Spatiales et Radio/Centre de Contrôle des Fréquences" (CNMOTSR/CCF), its counterpart of the F9TM CW station. It is active since at least 1934, and it is still active.
More informations at: https://web.r-e-f.org/le-reseau-f9tm-et-fav22/
