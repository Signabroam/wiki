About PBB Stanag-4285

Name: PBB Stanag-4285
Signabroam ID: MD42-01
Frequencies 5160, 6498, 6893, 12568 kHz
Status Active
Voice Unknown
Mode USB, MIL-STD-4285
Location Den Helder, The Netherlands
Known counterpart stations PBB, PCB, PCB Stanag-4285, Dutch Navy Stanag-4285
Description The PBB4285 are a stanags modem used by the Dutch Navy, its counterpart of the PBB RTTY station.
