About PCB Goeree Island

Name: PCB Goeree Island
Signabroam ID: TTY-04-2
Frequencies 4280, 5667, 6358.5, 8439, 12577, 12840.7 kHz
Status Active
Voice N/A
Mode USB, RTTY 75/850
Location Goeree Island, The Netherlands
Known counterpart stations PCB Stanag-4285, Dutch Navy Stanag-4285, PBB Stanag-4285, PBB Den Helder
Description PCB Goeree Island is a RTTY station used by the Dutch Navy, its counterpart of the PBB Den Helder RTTY station. It is active since at least 1965, and it is still active.
