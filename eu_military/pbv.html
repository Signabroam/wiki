About PVB NAS Valkenburg

Name: PVB NAS Valkenburg
Signabroam ID: D01
Frequencies 3128, 5705, 6728, 8971, 11178 kHz
Status Active
Voice N/A
Mode USB
Location NAS Valkenburg, The Netherlands
Known counterpart stations PCB Stanag-4285, PBB Den Helder, Dutch Navy Stanag-4285, PBB Stanag-4285, PCB Stanag-4285
Description PVB NAS Valkenburg is a counterparty station used by the Dutch Navy, its counterpart of the PBB Den Helder and PCB Goeree Island RTTY stations. It is active since at least 1965, and it is still active.
