About PBB Den Helder

Name: PBB Den Helder
Signabroam ID: TTY-04-1
Frequencies 2474, 4250, 4280, 5214, 5052, 5659, 5694.5, 6356, 6358.5, 8435.4, 8437, 8439, 9264, 9368, 12840.5, 12841, 16318, 17116.7, 17947, 18308, 20098 kHz
Status Active
Voice N/A
Mode USB, RTTY 75/850
Location Den Helder, The Netherlands
Known counterpart stations PCB, PCB Stanag-4285, Dutch Navy Stanag-4285, PBB Stanag-4285
Description PBB Den Helder is a RTTY station used by the Dutch Navy, its counterpart of the PCB RTTY station. It is active since at least 1965, and it is still active.
