Description This unidentified Russian station was first heard on May 24th, 2026 on 9062 kHz.
It use same low tone pip marker as The Squeaky Wheel, it is transmitting from far eastern Russia, we dont have more informations about it yet...
It got also heard on 7121 kHz, and a pip marker was also heard on 11531 kHz, the frequencies looks changing every day, same of the callsigns.
The station is active and it is transmitting in USB mode, the voices on the station are both male and female. It transmit mainly Net Check similar to DMT format.
Also the Pikhta26 station has been identified as a potential counterpart, the actual marker is sometime audible in the background.

- Update 27/05/2026: The day frequencies are constantly changing, but the night frequency is still the same (7121 kHz), also 12035 kHz didnt moved for 2 days.
