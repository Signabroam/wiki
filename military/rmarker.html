About The R Marker

Name: The R Marker
Callsigns: Plovets41
Frequencies 4325 kHz
Status Inactive
Voice Male, Female
Mode USB
Location Ekaterinburg, Russia
Known counterpart stations S28, S30, S32
Description Plovets41 is a Russian military central command station. According to radioscanner.ru it is located in Ekaterinburg and serves Central Military District of Russia. For the most of time it sends its channel marker - morse code R. Occasionally voice messages are sent mostly in Monolyth format.
Page of voice messages archives at : https://priyom.org/military-stations/russia/plovets-41/.
