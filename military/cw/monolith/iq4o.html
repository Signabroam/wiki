About IQ4O

Name: IQ4O
Callsigns: IQ4O, WTVL
Signabroam ID: MO05
Frequencies
Months Jan Feb Mar Apr May Jun Jul Aug Sep Oct Nov Dec
Daytime ? 7994 8543 ? ?
Nighttime 3366 ? ? ? 3366
Status Unknown
Mode OOK (1A1), CW
Location Russia
Known counterpart stations LRA, WTVL, The Buzzer (S28)
Description This station sends Monolit type signals in morse code adressed to the callsign IQ4O.
Some messages on the same frequencies are also adressed to WTVL. It is most active during days, on which messages on the channel of the Buzzer appear. Not much is known about this network.

All information above is credited from archives of priyom.org.
