About RICARJ

Name: RICARJ
Callsigns: RICARJ
Frequencies 5832 kHz
Status Active
Mode OOK (1A1), CW
Location Renchovo, Russia
Known counterpart stations Unknown
Description The station RICARJ is linked to the 72131/72132 units operating TU-22 aircraft from Renchovo airbase, which is part of the Long Range Aviation (LRA) branch of the Russian Air Force.

All information above is credited from radioreference.com.
