AboutKerro Pip

Name: Kerro Pip
Callsigns: N/A
Signabroam ID: U6218
Frequencies 4625, 5780, 5838, 5838.5, 6180, 6218, 6230, 6322, 6330, 6402, 6700, 6930, 8800, 8900, 9041, 13150, 16480
Status Inactive
Voice N/A
Mode USB
Location 60th Communications Hub, Kerro, Russia
Known counterpart stations The Buzzer Northern Russian Network Southern Russian Network The Russian XM Whales Feedback The Musical Pip
Description The Kerro Pip Russian Network is a propaganda network against Ukraine, which is believed to be operated by the Russian government. It is known for broadcasting propaganda songs such as Nationalist Russian songs against Ukraine, and it is often heard in the northern regions of Russia.
Those stations was operated by the same operator as The Buzzer, and they are believed to be counterparts to the Northern Russian Network, which also broadcasts propaganda songs against Ukraine.
