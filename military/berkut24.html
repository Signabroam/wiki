About Berkut24

Name: Berkut24
Callsigns: Berkut24, ''' (Znack Znack Znack)
Frequencies 3870, 5210
Status Active
Voice Male
Mode USB
Location Russia
Known counterpart stations N/A
Description This network was first heard in 2009, the first signal was caught in 2013. Not much known about this station, but presumably it's located somewhere in Southern Military District. 

Signal format on this station changed a lot of times:

In 2013: XXXX Word XXXX
In 2023: XXXXX Word XXXXX
From 2024: XXXXX Word XXXX
                
Where X is any digit.
Page of voice messages at : https://priyom.org/military-stations/russia/berkut24.
