About FGKT

Name: FGKT
Callsigns: FGKT, 'GOR (ьГОР)
Signabroam ID: S6970
Frequencies 5400, 6970
Status Active
Voice Male
Mode USB
Location 69th Communication Hub, Naro-Fominsk, Russia
Known counterpart stations Unit 83320 Buzzer CIS-3
Description "FGKT" / "ФГКТ" is a Russian military commandment network serving the Moscow Military District from the 69th Communitcation Hub at Naro-Fominsk. It broadcasts around the clock on 6970 kHz and 5400 kHz.
Page of voice messages at : https://priyom.org/military-stations/russia/fgkt.
