About S4574

Name: Slavic Call: S4574
Callsigns: Unidentified yet
Signabroam ID: S4574
Frequencies 4574 kHz
Status Active
Voice Male, Female
Mode DSB (USB and LSB at same time), Voices are stronger in USB otherwise the GroundLoop is stronger in LSB
Location Russia or Belarus
Known counterpart stations Supposely S4468...
Description S4574 is a unknown Russian station first heard in May 2026, its transmitting in DSB (which is rare) and mostly transmit Net Checks and DMT format messages.
Callsign has not been identified yet, but we have records and we may identify it in the future.
