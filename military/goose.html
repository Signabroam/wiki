About The Goose

Name: The Goose
Callsigns: Ohvat98
Frequencies 3243, 4310
Status Inactive
Voice Male, Female
Mode USB
Location Smolensk, Russia
Known counterpart stations The Alarm, The Air Horn
Description "The Goose" is a Russian military commandment network serving the Western Military District. It broadcasts on 4310 kHz during daytime, changing to 3243 kHz for nighttime.
Page of voice messages at : https://priyom.org/military-stations/russia/the-goose.
