About Proselok

Name: Proselok
Frequencies 6685, 8847, 11360, 18030 kHz
Status Active
Voice Male, Female
Mode USB
Location 334th Regiment, Pskov, Russia
Known counterpart stations LRA
Description Proselok is a Russian air transport station operated by the 334th Regiment, based in Pskov. It is known for its distinctive voice transmissions. The station has been observed transmitting on frequencies 6685 kHz and 11360 kHz primarily, in USB mode. The content of the transmissions often includes weather reports, flight instructions, and other operational communications relevant to air transport activities.
