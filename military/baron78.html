About Baron78

Name: Baron78
Callsigns: Tros28, Baron78
Frequencies 3850, 4940
Status Inactive
Voice Male, Female
Mode USB
Location Russia
Known counterpart stations S30, S32, Vega
Description Baron78 (Барон78) is the operating callsign of the central hub of a Russian military commandment network serving the Southern Military District. It operates on 4940 kHz during daytime and 3850 kHz during evening/nighttime making use of transmitter noise as a channel marker. It is a station in the same network as The Pip, The Squeaky Wheel and Vega.
Page of voice messages at : https://priyom.org/military-stations/russia/baron-78.
