About Voice Communication Propagation Test

Name: Voice Communication Propagation Test
Callsigns: Multiple
Signabroam ID: S6981
Frequencies 4626.5, 5023.5, 5106, 6800.5, 6981, 7211.5, 7935 kHz
Status Active
Voice Male, Female
Mode USB
Location Russia
Known counterpart stations Unknown
Description Russian military shown conducting specific user HF voice circuit checks, shown during 1430 UTC.
Simulcast frequencies of 6981 kHz and 7935 kHz are active as of 1 Sep 2025 1430 UTC; 4626.5 & 6800.5 kHz as of 15 Nov 2023.
The signal will start with an 800Hz control circuit activation, followed by the call sign of the target user repeated three times,
then a numbers count from 1 to 10 followed by a request for signal reception strength relayed via alternate communication call back.
This signal began on 24 March as drone attacks into Ukraine became a normal part of military activities, alternate frequency 7211.5 kHz only used sparingly with day and night time use, typical transition had occurred at ~1700 UTC some voice announcements occur at ~1430 UTC.
Old night time frequency is 5023.5 / 5106 kHz with voice bursts at 1930UTC.

Informations and descriptions from Tyler Stampfli 'KA0KA' on YouTube @SignalPhantom for SignalPhantom.com

There likely the same stations in Eastern Far of Russia with this format, see S9062 and S12035 for more information.
