About Zaliv52

Name: Zaliv52
Callsigns: Prizma60, Zaliv52
Signabroam ID: S4110
Frequencies 4110
Status Active (Occasional)
Voice Male, Female
Mode USB
Location Moscow, Russia
Known counterpart stations N/A
Description "Zaliv52" is a Russian military commandment network serving the Western Military District. It broadcasts on 4110 kHz without channel marker. First heard in 2017.

The traffic consists mainly of "Monolith" format messages and "МОЛНИЯ 5X" messages addressed to Залив52 (Zaliv52).
Page of voice messages at : https://priyom.org/military-stations/russia/zaliv-52.
